Welcome to the game board! Your goal is to find all the matching pairs of typefaces. To do this, click on the first card to turn it over and reveal the typeface it's hiding. Then, click on a second card to try and find its partner. If the two typefaces are the same, the pair is found and the cards will remain face up. If they are different, they will automatically turn face up after a short time.
